Division game

Division Game: There are 2N gold coins sitting on a table, of different weights. Alice and Bob know all the weights, and they are going to split these coins up between themselves so they each get N coins. The way they will do so is as follows. Alice first picks a coin on the table, say x, Bob chooses who gets to keep x (him or Alice). Then Bob picks another coin y on the table, now Alice chooses who gets to keep y, and so on back and forth, until one player has N coins, at which point all the remaining coins go to the other player. Each player wants to maximise the sum of the weights of the coins that they get. It seems to be true, but we don’t yet know how to prove, that Bob is always better off than Alice in this game!
